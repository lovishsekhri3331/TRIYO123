Creating a workplace environment of transparency, ownership, and accountability, to empower managers and their teams.

Our Story

Once upon a time, an entrepreneur witnessed a major problem: as companies scaled rapidly, they struggled to keep employees engaged, productive, and happy. An effective, transparent management tool was desperately needed.

The idea? Create a team management solution to connect people to workplace processes, for companies of all sizes, across any industry. But don’t just make a tool that works, make a tool that people love to use. Today, triyosoft.com workplace tool for more than 70,000 teams around the world who are now more engaged, more collaborative, and more on top of their work than ever before.

Triyosoft.com on the belief that transparency and collaboration create a culture of ownership and empowerment; more empowered employees are more productive employees. And we’re only scratching the surface of what we can accomplish together.

Meet our Team

We’re a team of passionate and experienced professionals rom all walks of life. Though our backgrounds couldn’t be ore diverse (especially once you get to know us!), we’re united y our shared mission to create software that simplifies your worklife. And why not? Have a lot of fun along the way.
